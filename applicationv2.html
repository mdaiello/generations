Why Volunteer at Generations?

Did you know that 40% of senior citizens regularly experience loneliness, according to a University of California, San Francisco (UCSF) study? This regular feeling of loneliness and disconnection can lead to severe health problems, and here at Generations it is our mission to mitigate elderly loneliness. Generations prioritizes connections and reducing the rate of loneliness amongst senior citizens. While many services charge fees for their services, we at Generations do not charge anything. We want to help students serve their communities and reward them with volunteer hours. We also want to create long-lasting connections with all users, as we are much more than a company, more a family. Here at Generations, connections drive change, drive innovation, and bring our communities together!

Benefits

Active Service to Your Community

By volunteering with Generations, you are actively serving your community and supporting an amazing cause. You are part of something that helps others during these unprecedented times.

Volunteer Hours

As a Generations Volunteer, you receive quick and easy volunteer hours while helping and engaging with others. These hours can be used towards your school's requirements.

Flexible and Local

Generations is local and flexible as we work to match you with seniors without forcing you to have to change your schedule.
