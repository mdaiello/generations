Welcome to Generations

Come for a job, leave with a connection

Why Apply to Generations?

Generations is an online platform that allows senior citizens to post yard work jobs such as lawn mowing and snow shovelling for students to complete in exchange for volunteer hours. Our mission is to bridge the gap between two distinct generations through community service, hence the name Generations. Here at Generations, we value intergenerational connections and hope to inspire the youth who lead tomorrow. By creating a website, we at Generations hope to foster connections and mitigate loneliness amongst seniors which has become increasingly prevalent amidst the pandemic. We also hope to help students find volunteering opportunities and help benefit their community.

Our Services

Yard Work

Want your lawn mowed or snow shovelled? Well, you're in luck. Senior citizens can request our volunteers to come to their house and take care of their yard work needs.

Tech Support

Having issues with your electronic device or need help setting it up? Well, look no further, because the Generations Volunteers will help you will all of your tech needs.

Gen2Gen Conversations

Don't need any yard work or tech help and just want to chat? Request a time to meet with one of our Volunteers for a chat and fun activities.

How We Started

Amidst the pandemic, a connection has been lost and Generations is committed to bringing it back. Inspired by our community we set out to make a change and help others during these unprecedented times. We did this through the creation of the Generations website!

We Are Local

Our service is quick, easy, and cost-free. We are committed to serving our customers with care and sincerity! We also pride ourselves on being local in the North Toronto and Bedford Park area, so we are always going to be there for you.

Our COVID-19 Plan

While others may see Covid-19 as a loss, we at Generations see it as an opportunity. We see the pandemic as an opportunity to foster virtual connections that will continue post-pandemic. We have strict guidelines in place to ensure that both our Volunteers and you stay safe.
