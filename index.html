About


Generations is an online platform that allows senior citizens to post yard work jobs such as lawn mowing and snow shovelling for students to complete in exchange for volunteer hours. Our mission is to bridge the gap between two distinct generations through community service, hence the name Generations. Here at Generations, we value intergenerational connections and hope to inspire the youth who lead tomorrow.



By creating a website, we at Generations hope to foster connections and mitigate loneliness amongst seniors which has become increasingly prevalent amidst the pandemic. We also hope to help students find volunteering opportunities and help benefit their community.
