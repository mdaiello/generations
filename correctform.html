About


Generations prioritize connections and reducing the rate of loneliness amongst senior citizens. While many services charge fees for their services, we at Generations do not charge anything. We want to help students serve their communities and reward them with volunteer hours. We also want to create long-lasting connections with all users, as we are much more than a company, more a family. Here at Generations, connections drive change, drive innovation, and bring our communities together!
